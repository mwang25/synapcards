What is SynapCards?

Synapcards is a personal information storage, retrieval, and sharing system. It uses two familiar things from the physical world: neural synapses and index cards. You can read more in the about page.

This website is in a very preliminary state. Eventually you will be able to add your own cards to your own account, search them, and share them with whomever you want. For now, I will show the most recently added top-rated cards on this page, and some featured cards on my profile page.
