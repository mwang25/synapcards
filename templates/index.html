What is SynapCards?

Synapcards is an information storage, retrieval, and sharing system. It uses two familiar things from the physical world: neural synapses and index cards. You can read more in the about page and the release notes.

This website is under development. A limited number of people can create their own account on Synapcards (by signing in) and add a limited number of cards. Currently, all cards are public, meaning they are visible to everyone. Eventually you will be able to control who can see your cards (everyone/public, shared, or private/only you).

Next on the todo list is the ability to follow users and get email notifications when that user adds or edits his/her cards.
